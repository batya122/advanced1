Our Story

At Bavaria, our story begins within the vibrant halls of Astana IT University, where our three co-founders, Sultangali Beibituly Kozhanov, Maksotov Ali Ruslanuly, and Zhukabayev Batyrkhan Tulepbergenovich, embarked on a remarkable journey of entrepreneurship. United by a profound fascination for cutting-edge technology and a deep love for automobiles, our founders recognized the untapped potential of Chinese car manufacturers. This realization laid the cornerstone for Bavaria, where we've combined our expertise, determination, and dedication to excellence to offer you the best Chinese cars on the market.
